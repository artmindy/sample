Fin App

2020

A finance controlling app that keeps all your assets in one place and lets you earn on expenses, makes mvoney transfer on the go.

Roles

User research, user interview, wireframing, prototyping, visual design

Problems defined

Users usually have several bank cards from different banks and have to manage between them to get info on their overall balance, to decide which card to use better while making various transactions for shopping and other activities.

1.Much applications

Almost every bank has their own bank application that differs from each other (making users remember every time what possibilities are provided by each app and the way to access them).

2.Overall balance?

Moreover users must manage by ourselves to stay up to dated with their overall balance from different bank cards.

3.Top services

Some bank applications do not provide the possibilities that are provided by others.

Findings

1.Top applications. Users ranked those as the most wanted: balance management, everyday transactions as different payments, money transfer, cashback from shoppings, stock trading options

2.Right one. Most users do not have adequate financial and juridical literacy to choose the most appropriate application. Most users choose to pick the application from the App Store or Play Market, where they believe the applications undergo some kind of control.

3.Security. Users outline that the risk associated with relying on third party applications for making operations and management of their assets are high.

4.Easy to use. Users ranked the following factors as most important when deciding which application to use: less private information to entry, simplification of login with secured control, free trial with beforehand notifications on withdrawals, comprehensive info on how to use application and it’s main features.

How might we help users securely manage their money from different bank cards while giving the best of each bank’s service?

Explorations

The power of integration. By combining the top ranked services from different banks and presenting them in one unified way. This will benefit users as their operations are secured and at another side the banks will benefit by providing their services.

Exploring ideas that put the users’ need on money related transactions at the forefront. Learning the highest ranked need for users to concentrate the top ranked services from different bank applications are pivotal moments for the project alongside with the security issues and overall reliability.

Core functions

Findings from users’ interviews and testing helped define the main features of the MVP

1.Multiple services from different banks. Users need the ability to use the most important and daily used functions from other bank services.

2.Simplify. To make the application easy to use and simplify all processes without even showing the user the complicated transaction details or savings schemas.

3.Saving while shopping. Users want to get more profits from expenses on shopping from different shops and e-commerce platforms.

The Sight Center

2020

The design of web site developed for the ophthalmic clinic that delivers medical and optics services.

Roles

Visual design, wireframing, prototyping

Moodboard

The color pallete was selected to communicate the joyfulness and the brightness by colorful but calming tones with white undertones that represent cleanliness and accuracy in medicine. Green tone creates peace and harmony as the healt is the prominent condition for human beings. Orange tones injects pleasure, energy and desire elements that must be associated with the improved health, especially with sight problems. The gray is used to balance out colorful tones of orange and pink to bring some calm and conservatives as the medicine itself is more about reliable and verifiable approach.

Based on the color palette chosen the moodboard was built to represent clients requirements and wishes as the baseline for the website design. Client’s view was to create welcoming and bright website and interior design where the good mood and positiveness are prominent.
